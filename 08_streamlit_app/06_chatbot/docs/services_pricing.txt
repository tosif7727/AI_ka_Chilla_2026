Steel Pro Hardware Shop - Services & Pricing

=== CUTTING SERVICES ===

We offer professional steel cutting for all products purchased in-store.

1. Angle Grinder Cut (Manual)

   - Accuracy: ±2mm
   - Price: $2.00 per cut
   - Turnaround: Same day (while you wait)

2. Cold Saw Cut (Precision)
   - Available for: All bar, tube, pipe, and channel sections
   - Accuracy: ±0.5mm
   - Price: $3.50 per cut
   - Turnaround: Same day (while you wait)

3. Plasma Cutting (Sheet & Plate)
   - Available for: Steel plate, sheet metal, flat bar
   - Accuracy: ±1mm
   - Price: $8.00 per cut (straight lines)
   - Custom shapes: Quoted on request
   - Turnaround: Same day for simple cuts, 1–2 days for custom shapes

4. Cutting to Length (Bulk Orders)
   - 10+ cuts of the same size: 20% discount on cutting price
   - Must be same material and same length
