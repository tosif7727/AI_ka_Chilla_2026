=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

Q: Do you sell to the public or only to trade?
A: We sell to everyone — public, tradies, and businesses alike. No trade account needed for walk-in purchases.

Q: Do you offer trade accounts?
A: Yes. Businesses can apply for a 30-day trade account. Requirements: ABN, two trade references, and a completed application form. Apply in-store or email accounts@steelprohardware.com.au. Approval takes 3–5 business days.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept cash, EFTPOS, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfer (EFT). Trade account holders can pay by invoice. We do not accept Amex or cheques.

Q: Can I get a quote before buying?
A: Yes. Call (03) 5555-7890, email info@steelprohardware.com.au, or come in-store. For custom fabrication jobs, bring your drawings or measurements and we will provide a free written quote.

Q: Do you cut steel to size?
A: Yes. We offer angle grinder cuts ($2.00), cold saw cuts ($3.50), and plasma cutting ($8.00+). See our Services & Pricing sheet for full details.

Q: What is the minimum length you will cut to?
A: Minimum cut length is 300mm for bar and tube sections, and 200mm for sheet/plate.

Q: Can I return or exchange steel I have already bought?
A: We accept returns on uncut, unmodified stock within 7 days with receipt. A 15% restocking fee applies on returns over $100. Cut-to-length or custom items cannot be returned. Faulty or incorrectly supplied items are replaced at no cost.

Q: Do you deliver?
A: Yes. Local delivery (within 20km) starts at $25. Free delivery on orders over $500 locally. See our Services & Pricing sheet for full delivery rates.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10am (local area only, +$15 surcharge). Standard local delivery is next business day. Regional delivery takes 1–3 business days.

Q: Do you carry stainless steel?
A: Yes. We stock Grade 304 stainless steel in round bar, flat bar, sheet, and tube. Grade 316 (marine grade) is available on order — 3–5 business day lead time.

Q: Do you stock aluminium?
A: Yes. We carry a basic range of aluminium flat bar, angle, square tube, and round tube. Ask in-store for current stock and pricing.

Q: What if the size I need is not in stock?
A: We can special order most steel sections. Lead time is typically 3–7 business days. A deposit of 50% is required on special orders. Minimum special order value is $150.

Q: Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?
A: Yes. Bulk pricing is available for orders over $1,000. Speak to our counter staff or call us for a bulk quote. Trade account holders receive an additional 5% discount on standard stock.

Q: Can you help me figure out what I need for my project?
A: Absolutely. Our staff are experienced and happy to advise on the right material, size, and grade for your job. Bring your plans, sketches, or measurements and we will help you work it out.

Q: Do you have a price list?
A: Pricing is available in-store and on our website. Prices are subject to change due to steel market fluctuations. We will always confirm the current price before processing your order.

=== SAFETY & HANDLING ===

Q: Is steel safe to handle without gloves?
A: We recommend always wearing heavy-duty gloves when handling steel. Edges and cut ends can be very sharp. Safety glasses are also recommended, especially near cutting operations.

Q: Do you provide Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS)?
A: Yes. MSDS sheets are available for all our products on request, either in-store or by email.
